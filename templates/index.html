About VISTA

In India, approximately 63 million individuals roughly 6.3% of the population are hard of hearing, a figure projected to rise due to an aging demographic. Although many in this community view their hearing status as a distinct way of life rather than a disability, they frequently encounter significant barriers to social interaction.

Our mission is to develop a robust and scalable system for the real-time translation of Indian Sign Language (ISL), accessible without requiring specialized hardware. We aim to bridge communication gaps through a seamless, bidirectional translation approach. Our primary focus is creating advanced models capable of converting spoken or written text into fluid, animated ISL gestures, while simultaneously developing the capability to accurately interpret dynamic ISL gestures and translate them back into readable text output.
